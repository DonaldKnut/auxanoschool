Admissions Information

Welcome to our admissions page. We're thrilled that you're considering Auxano Nursery and Primary School International for your child's educational journey.

Application Process

To apply for admission, please follow the steps below:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents, including:
    • Birth certificate
    • Immunization records
    • Previous school records (if applicable)
  3. Attend an admissions interview with our staff.
  4. Receive admission decision.

Admission Criteria

We welcome students of all backgrounds and abilities. Admission decisions are based on various factors, including:

  • Academic readiness
  • Personal qualities
  • Diversity considerations
