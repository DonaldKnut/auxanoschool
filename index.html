Welcome to Auxano International School, a nurturing haven for young minds to blossom and grow! Our Nursery and Primary School is dedicated to providing a vibrant and stimulating learning environment where each child's unique potential is recognized and celebrated. Here's a glimpse into what makes our school the perfect place for your child's educational journey:

Creche

we understand the importance of providing a nurturing environment for your little ones. Our creche offers a safe and loving space where infants are cared for by trained professionals who prioritize their well-being and development.

Pre School

There is a culture of a stong beliefxs in laying a strong foundation for lifelong learning. Our preschool program is designed to foster curiosity, creativity, and confidence in young children through engaging activities and hands-on experiences.

Elementary

we are committed to providing a dynamic and enriching educational experience for elementary-aged children. Our school cultivates a supportive and inclusive community where students are empowered to reach their full potential academically, socially, and emotionally.

Staff

Team of Awesome Educators

Our heartbeat is our incredible team of educators. Each member is carefully selected for their passion, expertise, and unwavering commitment to nurturing young minds. With a blend of experience and innovation, our staff brings learning to life in every classroom. From the warm embrace of our creche to the dynamic atmosphere of our elementary school, our staff is the driving force behind every child's journey to success.

Education

Art Inclined

Unleash the creativity within! Our arts and crafts activities allow students to express themselves through various artistic mediums. From painting and drawing to sculpture and crafts, we nurture the budding artists in our school.

Experiences

Hands-on Experiences

Through hands-on experiences, meaningful interactions, and personalized guidance, we lay the foundation for lifelong learning and success, one joyful moment at a time.

Explore

Mentorship Approach

As children transition to elementary school, our educators become mentors, igniting a love for knowledge that extends beyond the classroom walls. With enthusiasm and expertise, they empower students to think critically, explore boldly, and dream fearlessly.

Fostering

Unique Approach

We understand that every child is a unique individual with their own interests, strengths, and pace of development. That's why our approach to education revolves around fostering a love for learning through play, exploration, and discovery.
